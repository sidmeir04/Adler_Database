Our peer-to-peer tutoring program connects students with fellow learners who have mastered the subjects they need help with. This unique approach not only enhances understanding but also builds a supportive community of learners.

How It Works

Students are matched with peer tutors based on their academic needs, learning style, and personality. Our peer tutors are students who have excelled in their subjects and are passionate about helping others succeed.

Benefits of Peer-to-Peer Tutoring

Peer tutors are often more relatable to students because they share similar experiences and challenges. This relatability fosters a comfortable learning environment where students feel more at ease to ask questions and express difficulties.

Balancing school, extracurricular activities, and personal commitments can be challenging. That’s why we offer flexible scheduling options to ensure that quality tutoring fits seamlessly into your busy life.

Customizable Session Times

Our platform allows you to book tutoring sessions at times that work best for you. Whether it’s early in the school days, during lunch, or at the end of school. Life happens, and sometimes plans change. If you need to reschedule a session, our easy-to-use platform lets you adjust your appointment with minimal hassle, ensuring that you never miss out on valuable learning time.

Session Length Options

Choose from a variety of session lengths to match your learning needs and attention span. Opt for a quick, 10-minute review session or dive deeper with an period-long or even longer session for comprehensive understanding.

To support students in meeting their graduation requirements, we offer a comprehensive Community Service Tracking feature that helps you stay on top of your volunteer hours.

How It Works

Community Service hours will automaticly be tracked once a tutoring session has ended and you can monitor your progress towards the 60-hour community service requirement with our intuitive tracking dashboard. See a clear overview of your completed hours and what’s remaining, helping you stay motivated and organized.

Join Our Community
